Who is Stacy?

Stacy is an artist, programmer, and speaker.

She sees developing full-stack web applications as a creative endeavor. Her background in art has given her a unique perspective on solving technical problems. Her language of choice is Python, as it is versatile in building full scale backend solutions and small programs to run on microcontrollers.

As an artist, Stacy uses her programming knowledge to build lighting solutions for her photography. She will be presenting a talk at PyCon Australia 2018 entitled, Lighting Macro Photography with CircuitPython.

Stacy has spoken at PyCon Australia 2017 and PyCon 2018 (in Cleveland, OH) on the subject of code reviews. Links to those talks are below.

When she is not programming or building something, Stacy enjoys hiking and hanging out with her dog, Peanut.
