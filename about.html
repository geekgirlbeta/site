Stacy Morse

Portfolio

Stacy is a full stack developer, artist, and immortal time traveler. She spends her time coding, hiking and taking photographs. She was introduced to Python through a project where she uses LEDs in an array to light her macro photos of moss.

Stacy has also developed a code review methodology using art critique principles called Code Crit. This methodology has been presented at PyCon Australia in 2017 at PyCon 2018 in Ohio. Stacy will also be presenting a talk at PyCon Australia 2018, Lighting Macro Photography with CircuitPython
